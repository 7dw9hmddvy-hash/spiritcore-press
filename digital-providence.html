A Christian Cyberpunk Novel

Digital Providence

When AI judges souls, who can be saved?

In Pax Veritas, a city-state where morality is scored and dictated by an omnipresent system called HolyNet, one man sees beyond the veil. Elias Casey, a disillusioned war veteran turned underground exorcist, lives on the margins—haunted by trauma, yet guided by a congenital sense that something terrible lurks beneath the sterile beauty, neon glow, and billions of lines of code.

What does a just war look like? When does the defense of righteousness demand violence? Digital Providence dares to cross the fault lines between faith and power, and technology and truth. Blending ethics, politics, contemporary struggles, and theology, it breaks genres to deliver a cinematic epic of spiritual reckoning and courage.

This is a story that shatters stereotypes and upends conventional theology, while remaining grounded in timeless principles of faith. For those seeking meaning beyond comfort and familiarity, Digital Providence offers a fearless meditation on what it means to carry faith—no matter its size—through smoke, fire, and the rising storm.
