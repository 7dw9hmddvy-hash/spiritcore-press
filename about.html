Founder & Author

Kane W O’Brien

K

Kane W O’Brien is a thirty year old Christian Canadian who lives in Edmonton, Alberta. Born in Victoria, British Columbia to a prison guard and a welder, he spent his childhood moving between cities and towns throughout BC, playing competitive sports.

Although always an avid reader, he didn’t begin creative writing until his sophomore year of university at the Royal Military College of Canada, where he began writing poetry. After dropping out in his senior year of studies, he travelled the country, hustling where he could, and writing short stories and poetry along the way.

After recovering from a severe illness by the grace of God, Kane wrote Digital Providence in 2025. The work is a culmination of his studies in creative writing, politics, geopolitics, Christian theology, strategy, sociology, and military history, and is a reflection of his own walk with Christ.

In 2026, upon publication, Kane was working as a combo truck operator in an open pit mine.
