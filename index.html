Digital Providence

“What happens when a civilization can measure everything except the human soul?”

In Pax Veritas, every citizen lives beneath the judgment of HolyNet. Morality is scored. Faith is administered. Dissent is erased.

But when former Sanctum Guard operator Elias Casey begins seeing what the system cannot, he is drawn into a struggle involving an heiress vigilante, a secret syndicate, a trio of AI Judges, and a government that has mistaken control for providence.
